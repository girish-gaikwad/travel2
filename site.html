Angel Falls, Venezuela.

Why Venezuela?

Kerepakupai merú Pemon language: Kerepakupai Merú meaning "waterfall of the deepest place", Received the name of ”Angel Falls” after the american adventurer James Angel landed on its plane in 1937 The waterfall drops over the edge of the Auyán-tepui mountain in the Canaima National Park , a UNESCO World Heritage Site in the Gran Sabana region of Bolívar State.

Cappadocia, Turkey

Why Turkey?

Hot Air Balloon Ride: Witness the breathtaking landscapes of Cappadocia from a hot air balloon. It's an iconic experience, especially during sunrise. Göreme Open-Air Museum: Explore the ancient rock-cut churches and monasteries with stunning frescoes dating back to the Byzantine era. Fairy Chimneys: Visit Pasabag (Monks Valley) and Devrent Valley to see these unique rock formations created by volcanic eruptions.
